Mental Health x Music

The Music & Mental Health Survey Dataset comes from Kaggle and was compiled by Catherine Rasgaitis. These data were collected via a survey about music listening habits and their connection to mental health, that respondents filled out via Google Forms. The form was posted on different social media platforms such as Reddit and Discord. Posters and cards were distributed to advertise the survey in libraries, parks, and other public locations. Overall, the dataset contains 737 participants and 33 variables. This dataset consists of self-reported data, thus the reliability of the data cannot be confirmed.
